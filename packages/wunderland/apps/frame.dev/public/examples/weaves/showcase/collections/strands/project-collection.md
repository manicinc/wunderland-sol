# Project Collections Pattern

Collections shine when organizing everything related to a specific project, regardless of where individual strands live in your knowledge base.

## Example: Product Launch Collection

For a product launch, gather:

- **Specs** from `engineering/specs`
- **Designs** from `design/mockups`
- **Marketing copy** from `marketing/content`
- **Meeting notes** from `meetings/2024`
- **Research** from `research/user-interviews`

All accessible from one visual dashboard!
