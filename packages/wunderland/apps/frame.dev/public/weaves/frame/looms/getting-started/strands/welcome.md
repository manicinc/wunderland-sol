# Welcome to Quarry

Quarry is your personal knowledge management system that weaves together your notes, documentation, and ideas into an interconnected fabric of knowledge.

## What Makes Quarry Different?

### 1. OpenStrand Architecture

Your knowledge is organized using the OpenStrand schema:

- **Weaves** - Top-level knowledge universes (like folders)
- **Looms** - Categories within weaves (subfolders)
- **Strands** - Individual documents (markdown files)

### 2. Plain Markdown Files
