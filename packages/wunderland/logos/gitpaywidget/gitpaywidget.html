Usage Guidelines

Clear Space: Maintain minimum clear space equal to the height of the dollar sign icon around the logo.

Minimum Size: Never display the logo smaller than 120px wide (horizontal) or 80px wide (vertical).

Color Usage: Use the primary purple gradient for main brand touchpoints. Ocean, sunset, and forest variants for specific contexts.

Do Not: Stretch, rotate, add effects, change colors outside the palette, or place on busy backgrounds without proper contrast.
