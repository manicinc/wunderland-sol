Usage Guidelines

Minimum Size

Square logo: 100×100px minimum

Clear Space

Maintain clear space equal to 25% of logo height on all sides

Applications

Use square format for: app icons, social media profiles, favicons, PWA icons

Color Usage

Black or blue for professional contexts. Colored variants for marketing materials.
